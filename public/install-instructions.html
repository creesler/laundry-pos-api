Install Laundry King POS

Laundry King POS can be installed on your device for a better experience. Follow the instructions below for your browser:

Chrome (Desktop)

  1. Click the install icon (⊕) in the address bar
  2. Click "Install"
  3. The app will open in a new window

Chrome (Android)

  1. Tap the menu button (⋮)
  2. Tap "Add to Home screen"
  3. Tap "Add" to confirm

Safari (iOS)

  1. Tap the share button (□↑)
  2. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  3. Tap "Add" to confirm
Note: After installation, you can launch the app from your device's home screen or app launcher.
